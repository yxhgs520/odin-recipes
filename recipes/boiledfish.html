Boiled Fish

Description

Boiling is a healthy and quick way to prepare fish, preserving its natural flavor and delicate texture. This recipe features fresh fish fillets poached to perfection with simple aromatics.

Ingredients

  • 500g fresh fish fillets (such as cod, tilapia, or snapper)
  • 2 slices of fresh ginger
  • 1 stalk of green onion, cut into sections
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • Water (enough to submerge the fish)
  • A dash of light soy sauce and sesame oil for garnish

Steps

  1. Fill a large pan or pot with enough water to cover the fish and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add ginger slices, green onions, and salt to the boiling water.
  3. Gently place the fish fillets into the water.
  4. Lower the heat to a simmer and cook for about 5-8 minutes until the fish is opaque and flakes easily.
  5. Carefully remove the fish from the water and place it on a serving plate.
  6. Drizzle with soy sauce and sesame oil if desired before serving.
